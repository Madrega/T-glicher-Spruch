## Packages
date-fns | Date formatting and manipulation for the date picker and list
framer-motion | Smooth animations for list items and page transitions
lucide-react | Icons for the UI (already in base, but emphasizing usage)
react-day-picker | Calendar component for selecting dates

## Notes
The backend expects dates as "YYYY-MM-DD" strings for the `displayDate` field.
The RSS feed will be available at `/feed` (served by backend).
App uses a clean, minimal admin dashboard aesthetic.
